P:System.DateTime.Today;Use TimeProvider.GetLocalNow() instead
P:System.DateTimeOffset.Now;Use TimeProvider.GetLocalNow() instead
P:System.DateTimeOffset.UtcNow;Use TimeProvider.GetUtcNow() instead
